About Me

About

Growing up as the youngest single child in the family, I was always challenged by my two older cousins.

Despite the relative closeness, I was entertained by the stark differences displayed in the way they each carried themeselves both socially and academically.

The whole ideal child versus black sheep scenario, with me in the middle looking up to both for equal guidance. Yet here I was, wondering what my next adventure would be. Consequently, this led to an act first then learn from it later from both scenarios, which is why you're even reading this rather personal story.

The voyage of early childhood experiences that molded me to jump before the reality of that might not be a good idea seeped in (though I never broke a bone) defines me.

This is part of the reason why I am here. To keep learning because liberation to me is feeling prepared, even though the house is on fire.
